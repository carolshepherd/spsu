How to Play

Features of the cards

BeSet is based on cards and you are trying to creating sets from those cards. The cards have 4 features: Color, Shape, Shading, and Number.

What is a set?

A set consists of three cards which satisfy all of these conditions:

  • They all have the same number, or they have three different numbers.
  • They all have the same symbol, or they have three different symbols.
  • They all have the same shading, or they have three different shadings.
  • They all have the same color, or they have three different colors.

We use the same mechanics as Set so here is a great tutorial on how to make a set.

The Big Blue Bar

You might Notice a big blue bar on the left side of the screen and wonder why it is draining. That bar holds the Sand of Time, which drain out of the container until it reaches the bottom and the Goddess moves and selects a set.
